## Procedurally generated fictional maps

The files below are fictional equirectangular maps generated by
`scripts/generate-textures.mjs`. They are original content (no external
source) and can be regenerated at any time by re-running the script.

| File             | Used for archetype | Theme                            |
|------------------|--------------------|----------------------------------|
| poison.png       | poison             | toxic swamp — purple + acid green|
| amethyst.png     | amethyst           | violet crystalline world         |
| ember.png        | ember              | obsidian with glowing fissures   |
| oceanic.png      | oceanic            | deep-blue water world + clouds   |
| verdant.png      | verdant            | lush jungle with river veins     |
| desert.png       | desert             | rust-and-sand barren planet      |

Every planet in the game picks one of the archetypes deterministically from
its seed; the baker in `src/game/render/planetAssets.ts` re-projects the
equirectangular map onto a lit 2D disc at runtime.
